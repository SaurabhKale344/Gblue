Our Complete DEF Product Range

From portable buckets to bulk dispensing solutions, Gblue ensures you have the certified quality DEF you need, wherever you operate.

Packaged Solutions

20 Litres Bucket

The ideal choice for medium fleets and high-frequency vehicle operators. Our 20-litre packaging provides maximum value and ensures you meet all required emission standards without frequent reordering. The ergonomic design ensures clean and efficient pouring directly into your vehicle's DEF tank, minimizing spillage and waste while guaranteeing the certified purity of ISO 22241 standard fluid.

10 and 5 Litres Buckets

The perfect solution for individual vehicle owners, service garages, and emergency refills. The 10-litre bucket offers a great balance of size and value, while the compact 5-litre bucket is essential for on-the-road compliance. Both portable sizes feature easy-pour nozzles and tamper-proof seals, ensuring that the DEF remains contaminant-free and ready for immediate use, protecting your SCR system from impurities.

High-Volume Supply

210 Litres Barrels

Our 210 Litre HDPE barrels are designed for agricultural businesses, construction sites, and mid-sized fleet depots requiring consistent supply without the infrastructure needed for large bulk tanks. The robust, sealed packaging prevents contamination and is perfectly suited for use with manual or electric pumping kits, offering a reliable and manageable high-volume solution that safeguards the integrity of the DEF.

IBC Tanks (1000 Litres)

The standard solution for dedicated fleet managers and large industrial operations. Our 1000 Litre Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) provide a cost-effective and space-efficient way to store and dispense large volumes of DEF. The tanks are delivered ready-to-use and compatible with all standard dispensing systems, ensuring a controlled, efficient, and continuous supply stream for high-consumption environments.

Marine DEF / AUS 40

Gblue can produce specialized Marine Grade DEF (AUS 40 or 40% Urea solution), which meets the high concentration requirements essential for vessels operating under IMO Tier III emission regulations. Our certified Marine DEF is suitable for maritime SCR systems, ensuring compliance and maximizing operational time at sea. We are fully equipped and ready to supply DEF for ships, large vessels, and all marine transports, offering flexible supply options tailored to port logistics and bunker demands.
