What is DEF?

DEF stands for Diesel Exhaust Fluid, a non-toxic, colorless, odorless, and non-flammable fluid that's injected into the exhaust system of diesel vehicles to reduce harmful emissions. It's also known as AdBlue.

KNOW MORE

Why DEF is Important?

Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) is a key component in diesel engines because it helps reduce harmful emissions and ensure that the vehicle meets emission standards. It achieves a remarkable 85 - 95% reduction in NOx emissions.
